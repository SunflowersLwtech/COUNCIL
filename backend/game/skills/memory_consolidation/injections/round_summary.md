When summarizing a round, structure your memory around:
1. KEY ACCUSATIONS: Who accused whom, and was there evidence behind it?
2. VOTING ALIGNMENT: Did anyone vote against the group consensus? Who voted together consistently?
3. BEHAVIORAL SHIFTS: Did anyone change their tone, strategy, or alliances this round?
4. UNRESOLVED QUESTIONS: What contradictions or mysteries remain heading into the next round?
Distill these into 2-3 concise conclusions that will inform your strategy going forward.
